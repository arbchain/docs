## Entities

Entities of Arbchain are all the entities involved in the arbitration procedure.
They form the nodes in the Hyperledger Besu network. A few of the default nodes of Arbchain are:

* Arbitral court
* Registrar
* Claimant
* Respondant
* Tribunal
